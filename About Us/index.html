Welcome to RoboWhales

At RoboWhales Team 9032, we are dedicated to inspiring the next generation of innovators through robotics and STEM education. Our mission is to foster teamwork, creativity, and technical skills while engaging with our community and competing at the highest levels.

Discover the Journey and Achievements of RoboWhales Team 9032

Founded in 2022, RoboWhales Team 9032 has quickly made a mark in the robotics community. In their inaugural year, they proudly received the Autonomous Award at the FIRST North Carolina District State Championship, showcasing their commitment to innovation and excellence.
